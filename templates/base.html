How it works

1

Encode

On clicking on the encode button below, It allows you (a user) to select an image of your choice and provides a text field for inputing your secret message.

2

Download or Share

After encoding an image, A preview of the encoded image will be displayed with options like downloading and sharing.

3

Decode

After Encoding and saving has been done. To view a secret message in an encoded image you click on the decode button below, It also allows you (a user) to select a decoded image. And the secret message will be revealed with a copy button for saving in your clipboard.
